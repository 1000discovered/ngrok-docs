# ERR_NGROK_108

### Message
Your account is limited to `MAX` simultaneous ngrok agent session`PLURAL`.
`MSG`

### Further help
If you're having trouble resolving this error, please reach out to [support@ngrok.com](mailto:support@ngrok.com?subject=Help%20with%20ERR_NGROK_108)
<br>
Free or Personal ngrok plans support 1 agent. If you need more than 1, you can subscribe to a Pro plan or higher in your [billing settings](https://dashboard.ngrok.com/billing).
<br>
If you're on a paid plan and would like to purchase another agent, you can go to your [billing settings](https://dashboard.ngrok.com/billing) in the ngrok dashboard and enable overages in the toggle. Then, you will be able to add another agent to your account.
